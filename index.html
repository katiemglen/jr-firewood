Frequently Asked Questions

We primarily sell mixed hardwoods including oak, maple, birch, and ash. All wood is locally sourced from Dakota County and surrounding areas. We focus on species that burn hot and clean with good coaling properties.
Our seasoned firewood is split and dried for 6+ months in covered, ventilated stacks. We check moisture content to ensure it's below 20% for optimal burning. Properly seasoned wood burns hotter, cleaner, and with less smoke.
We deliver throughout Dakota County, MN and surrounding areas including Apple Valley, Lakeville, Burnsville, Eagan, and nearby communities. Free delivery on orders over $200. Small delivery fee for smaller orders.
Quarterly auto-delivery with 10% discount off regular prices. We deliver your usual order every 3 months. You can cancel, pause, or modify your subscription anytime. Great way to ensure you never run out of firewood.
Yes! Pickup available at our Dakota County location with advance notice. Call ahead to schedule and we'll have your wood ready for loading. Pickup saves on delivery fees.
Absolutely. We work with restaurants, parks, event venues, and businesses. Bulk pricing available for large orders. We can provide invoicing and work with your procurement process.
Stack wood off the ground on pallets or lumber. Cover the top but leave sides open for airflow. Keep 5+ feet away from structures to prevent pest issues. Well-stacked wood continues to dry and stays in better condition.
Seasoned wood is dried 6+ months, burns clean with less smoke and higher heat output. Green wood is fresh-cut, costs less but needs 6-12 months seasoning time before burning well. Choose based on when you need to burn it.
Basic neat stacking is included in delivery. Premium stacking service (tight stacking in specific location) available for an additional fee. Most customers prefer to stack themselves for best fit in their space.
